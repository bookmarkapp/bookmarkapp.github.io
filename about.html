Creating connections through the love of books

Bookmark is a dating app that was founded by avid readers who wanted to create a platform where book lovers can connect and find love based on their shared passion for literature. Inspired by the idea that books have the power to bring people together, Bookmark aims to create meaningful relationships through the love of books.
